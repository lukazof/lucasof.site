PureChessLib

PureChess is a chess library created in C#. Importing it into your project allows you to store chess games and moves. The library handles all aspects of creating the chessboard, move validation, and more.

Rede Império

Império (in english, Empire) is a network of Minecraft servers that I created a few years ago. It emerged as a continuation of "RedeChiqueiro," a server that a friend and I created. The server was quite popular, with over 500 registered unique members at one point.

Currently, Império is offline, but I plan to bring the servers back online in the near future.
